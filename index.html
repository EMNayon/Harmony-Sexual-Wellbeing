Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and sexual wellbeing.

1. Is my personal information kept confidential?
Yes, absolutely. We ensure 100% privacy and end-to-end encryption for all user data and consultation records.
2. How long does a counseling session last?
Most of our counseling sessions last between 30 to 45 minutes, depending on the complexity of the discussion.
3. Are the health quiz results accurate?
The quiz provides a preliminary assessment based on your inputs. For a definitive diagnosis, always consult a professional.
4. Can I consult anonymously?
Yes, we provide options for anonymous consultations where you can choose not to disclose your real name or face.
5. Is information scientifically verified?
All our articles and guides are reviewed by certified medical professionals and based on the latest scientific research.
6. Do you provide prescriptions?
We provide guidance. Prescriptions are only given during formal video or in-person consultations with our doctors.
7. Facing a sexual health crisis?
Please use our "Emergency Contact" button or visit the nearest healthcare facility immediately if you need urgent care.
8. Are there any hidden fees?
Browsing our blogs and taking basic quizzes is free. Fees only apply for private specialist appointments.
9. Stay updated with new health tips?
You can subscribe to our weekly newsletter by entering your email address in the subscription box below.
10. Share my success story?
Yes! You can submit your story through our "Success Stories" page. We will publish it anonymously upon your request.
